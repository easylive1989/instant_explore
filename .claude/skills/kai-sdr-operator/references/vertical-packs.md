# SDR Vertical Packs

Use vertical packs to set defaults. Do not treat them as facts about a specific client. Replace defaults with source-backed context as soon as the product, market, and account data are known.

## B2B SaaS

Default ICP:

- Company size: 10-500 employees unless product context says otherwise.
- Buyers: founder, VP Sales, RevOps, Marketing Ops, Product, Customer Success, or function owner.
- Signals: hiring for the problem area, tool migration, funding, new sales motion, new geography, public integration stack.

Primary sources:

- CRM exports.
- Product signup or trial data.
- Approved enrichment tools.
- Job posts, docs, pricing pages, integration directories, public case studies.

Disqualifiers:

- No clear business owner.
- Self-serve product with no sales-assisted motion.
- Heavy regulated-data use without review.

Default assets:

- ICP scorecard.
- Trial-to-meeting motion.
- Migration pain sequence.
- Meeting prep brief.

## Agency Or B2B Services

Default ICP:

- Companies with active spend, visible growth goals, or an expensive bottleneck.
- Buyers: founder, owner, VP Marketing, Head of Growth, RevOps, or channel owner.

Primary sources:

- Public site.
- Ad libraries when platform terms allow review.
- Search results and public channel activity.
- Referral lists and partner lists.

Disqualifiers:

- No proof the service category matters now.
- Price-point mismatch.
- Generic "we help businesses grow" positioning with no proof.

Default assets:

- Diagnostic audit offer.
- Competitor gap brief.
- Low-friction reply CTA.
- Sales call diagnostic questions.

## Local Phone-Led Services

Default ICP:

- Local firms where phone calls are a major conversion path.
- Buyers: owner, office manager, intake manager, practice manager, operations lead.
- Categories: legal, home services, clinics, dental, med spa, contractors, urgent local services.

Primary sources:

- Website phone path.
- Google Business Profile public facts.
- Public reviews.
- Call tracking or CRM exports when connected.
- After-hours coverage evidence from user or source-backed audit.

Disqualifiers:

- Self-serve-only workflow.
- Low call demand.
- Compliance unresolved.
- No source showing phone demand or user confirmation of phone-led sales.

Default assets:

- Missed-call risk brief.
- Speed-to-lead workflow.
- KaiCalls fit review with ownership disclosure.
- Alternative options comparison.

## Enterprise ABM SDR

Default ICP:

- Named accounts with high ACV and buying committee complexity.
- Buyers: economic buyer, champion, technical evaluator, procurement, legal, finance, blocker.

Primary sources:

- Public annual reports.
- Press releases.
- Job posts.
- Tech docs and integration pages.
- Existing CRM notes.
- User-provided account research.

Disqualifiers:

- No account-specific thesis.
- No mapped buyer.
- Generic sequence that could go to any company.

Default assets:

- Account dossier.
- Buying committee map.
- Trigger thesis.
- Multi-thread outreach plan.
- Executive meeting prep.

## Recruiting And Staffing

Default ICP:

- Employers hiring for roles the agency can fill or candidates matching an approved search.
- Buyers: talent acquisition, department head, founder, operations leader.

Primary sources:

- Public job posts.
- User-provided candidate or client data.
- CRM/ATS exports.
- Company careers pages.

Disqualifiers:

- Employment targeting risk unresolved.
- Sensitive personal data used without basis.
- Candidate contact source unclear.
- Pay, protected-class, or eligibility claims unsupported.

Default assets:

- Client demand brief.
- Role pain evidence.
- Candidate-introduction guardrails.
- Employment-policy review note.

## Partner And Channel SDR

Default ICP:

- Adjacent companies with shared audience, integration potential, co-marketing fit, referral fit, or marketplace fit.
- Buyers: founder, partnerships, BD, marketing, channel, product.

Primary sources:

- Partner directory.
- Integration marketplace.
- Public audience overlap.
- Co-marketing history.
- User-provided partner list.

Disqualifiers:

- No mutual value.
- Pure extraction ask.
- Competing channel conflict.
- No realistic joint offer.

Default assets:

- Partner fit matrix.
- Mutual value note.
- Co-marketing pitch.
- Referral workflow.

## Founder-Led Sales

Default ICP:

- Narrow founder-owned segment where the founder can credibly write from direct experience.
- Buyers: founder, operator, first functional owner.

Primary sources:

- Founder network.
- Product usage.
- Community interactions.
- Warm intros.
- Public context.

Disqualifiers:

- No founder point of view.
- Too broad a list.
- No meeting capacity.

Default assets:

- Founder POV brief.
- Warm intro script.
- One-question CTA.
- Founder meeting prep.
